Cybersecurity Learning Project (2026)

This project was developed as part of our studies in Robotics and Automation (2026) with the goal of gaining a deeper understanding of cybersecurity concepts through practical experimentation.

The initial premise is straightforward: establish a connection to a target machine and retrieve its IP address using an ESP32 or an Arduino configured as a Rubber Ducky–style device. From this foundation, the project will continuously evolve, incorporating more advanced techniques and expanding its scope as our knowledge grows.

Our objective is not only to explore the technical possibilities, but also to strengthen our skills and awareness in cybersecurity through hands-on learning and iterative development.
